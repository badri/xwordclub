{% trans "What's this site all about?" %}

A forum for solving The Hindustan Times cryptics.

{% trans "How does it work?" %}

Everyday, someone from the forum uploads the Hindustan times crossword clues as a new post.The junta start putting in the answers as a reply to that post. The usual procedure to post a reply is to copy the last clues post and enter our answers beside the unsolved clues. The answers need to be highlighted before posting the reply. This is done by selecting our answers and clicking the "B" icon on top of the reply box. Please refer to any of the existing crossword posts to get the hang of it.

{% trans "Do I have to annotate my answers?" %}

Yes. It helps newbies to understand and deconstruct the clues.

{% trans "I want to post the clues. How do I do it?" %}

Sure. Go to Hindustan Times, click on the "search" link. Search for the crossword. You'll get the concise and the cryptic crosswords. Click the cryptic and you'll get a printable image of the day's crossword. You can key in the clues you see there. A detailed procedure is given here. The convention for post title will be "HT-[ Grid number ] [ Date ] [ Month ] [ year ]". An example would be "HT-21773 5th March 2010".
