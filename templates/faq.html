{% trans "What's this site all about?" %}

A forum for solving The Hindustan Times cryptics.

{% trans "How does it work?" %}

Everyday, someone from the forum uploads the Hindustan times crossword clues and junta start putting in the answers.This is usually done in a piecemeal fashion similar to Orkut THC forum's posting conventions.

{% trans "Do I have to annotate my answers?" %}

Yes. It helps newbies to understand and deconstruct the clue, given the fact that HT cryptics have high standards.

{% trans "I want to post the clues. How do I do it?" %}

Sure. Go to Hindustan Times, click on the "search" link. Search for the crossword. You'll get the concise and the cryptic crosswords. Click the cryptic and you'll get a printable image of the day's crossword. You can key in the clues you see there. A detailed procedure is given here.
