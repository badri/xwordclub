This howto deals with posting answers to the daily crossword.

1.Clicking on the clue for a forum topic labelled "HT" or "MINT" opens a new window.

2.Post your answer, preferably with annotations in the text box. If for some reason you want the clue text in answer, click on the "copy clue to textbox" in bottom left.

3.You can delete your answer anytime by clicking the (x) next to your answer. Note that this operation has NO "undo". Apart from that, you can rate how tough the clue is(2), or rate others' answers(3). The karma points is a multipilicative factor of clue toughness and the number of upvotes the answer got.

4. By 8 p.m. IST everyday, all the answers entered for that clue will appear.
