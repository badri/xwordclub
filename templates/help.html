This howto deals with posting answers to the daily crossword.

1.Clicking on the clue for a forum topic labelled "HT" opens a new window.

2.Post your answer, preferably with annotations in the text box. Markdown syntax is accepted here.

3.You can delete your answer anytime by clicking the (x) next to your answer. Note that this operation has NO "undo".

4. By 8 PM everyday, all the answers entered for that clue will appear.
