What?

xwordclub is an online community for crossword enthusiasts. Feel free to ask any question pertaining to cryptic crosswords.

Who?

This site is run by users like you. Everyday, cryptic crosswords from various dailies are posted in the forum and discussed by them.

Why?

Mainly because there is no one stop in the web for general discussion of crosswords. xwordclub serves as a combination of a collaborative wiki + blog + forum for all things cryptic crossword.
