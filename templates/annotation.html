{% trans "Answers annotation guide" %}

xwordclub follows certains annotations in discussions while writing the answers. This is mainly intended for beginners who can't figure out what is the clue type otherwise.

{% trans "Anagrams" %}

indicated by a "*". Here's an example of how to do it.
Poor stewardship - mates naming me, unfortunately (13) MISMANAGEMENT*

{% trans "Homophones" %}

indicated by a "~" followed by the homophone word.
Star is lazy in audition (4) (~idle)IDOL

{% trans "Embedded constructions" %}

embedded word/letter is enclosed by "{}".
Get the answers to only about five (5) SOL{V}E Hear evidence about old disaster (7) TR{AGED}Y

{% trans "Telescopic clues" %}

indicated by "[T]" after the answer.
Deactivate part of lunar module (5) UNARM [T]

{% trans "Cryptic definitions" %}

indicated by "[CD]" after the answer.
The girl next door (9) NEIGHBOUR [CD]

{% trans "Double definitions" %}

indicated by "[DD]" after the answer.
Elevation popular with salaried workers (5) RAISE [DD]

{% trans "Additions, deletions, reverse etc" %}

indicated by +,- and <- respectively.
The latest thing in an initially fixed deposit with first rate return (7) {F}{ASH}{ION<-}
